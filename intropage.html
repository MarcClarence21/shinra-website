SHINRA ESPORTS

Shinra Esports is a gaming team founded by Marc Clarence Sangcap, Brian Osorio, and John Marvin Pasco. It is a multi-game esport, mainly Call of Duty.
This team also played Mobile Legends, Apex Legends, Clash of Clans, Wildrift, and Sausage Man.
